Hi, It's Hina Firdaus 👸

A passionate UX Designer and Researcher with a knack for creating user-centered solutions. My expertise spans UX Research, Usability Testing, UX Design, and Front-End Development, all driven by a deep understanding of human behavior and design principles.

I’m particularly focused on bridging technology with user needs, especially in areas like CSCW, CSCL, and Healthcare. Currently, I’m honing my craft at Fab Lab Siegen, where I’m immersed in innovative projects. When I’m not designing seamless experiences, you’ll find me swimming, cooking for friends, or exploring new places. Feel free to explore my work and projects. If you’re looking for someone to elevate your next UX project or collaborate on cutting-edge research, I’d love to connect!
