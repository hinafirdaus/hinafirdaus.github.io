Intro

I am happy you are here to learn about me. My biological and legal name is Hina Firdaus. Currently I am employed as a full time Assistant Professor in the Departement of Computer Science and Engineering at St. Andrews Institute of Technology and Management, Gurugram

I am Computer science and Engineering post graduate. Most of my educations were done in different part of India. I love to be called as Wonderer

My research area of interest and work is Machine learning for healthcare, Education Technology. In my free time I love to read books, watch movies, cook different cuisine, paint, sing in karaoke and record, vlog, bicycling.

Work

Major of my time in the week which is 6hr everyday and 5days per week which make it total of 30hr to teach and assess grad students mostly in the field of core computer science i.e., Python Programming, C programming, C++ programming, Data structures and Algorithm, Machine learning, Mobile communication etc.
